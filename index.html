The Michigan State Chapter of the American Institute of Graphic Arts

Connecting, Networking, Collaborating

We aim to celebrate design through connecting with students, networking with creative professionals, and collaborating through fun workshops and events. Although our group benefits design students the most, we urge students of any major to join!

About MSU AIGA

Mission

Our mission is to connect with one another and celebrate our passion for design through creative workshops and events. We invite creatives & designers of all majors to join us! We meet biweekly on Wednesdays at 7pm in the Kresge Art Center! Find more details on the schedule page.

AIGA

Michigan State AIGA is a chapter of the oldest and largest professional membership organization for design, AIGA. As part of the AIGA network, we bring these opportunities directly to our campus, connecting students with the broader design community. We are proud to represent an incredible organization and follow their value of design.
