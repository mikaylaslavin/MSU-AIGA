Connecting, Networking, Collaborating

We aim to celebrate design through connecting with students, networking with creative professionals, and collaborating through fun workshops and events. Although our group benefits design students the most, we urge students of any major to join! Check out our semester zines here!

About MSU AIGA

Our mission is to connect with one another and celebrate our passion for design through creative workshops and events. We invite creatives & designers of all majors to join us! We meet biweekly on Wednesdays at 7pm in the Kresge Art Center! Find more details on the schedule page.

Michigan State AIGA is a chapter of the oldest and largest professional membership organization for design, AIGA. We are proud to represent an incredible organization and follow their value of design.
